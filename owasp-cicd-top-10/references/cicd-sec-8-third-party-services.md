## Summary

Third-party apps and integrations (e.g. GitHub Apps, Slack) with broad permissions. Compromise or abuse of third party affects pipeline and repo.

## Prevention

- Approve and audit third-party integrations; grant minimum permissions. Review OAuth scopes and app access; revoke unused; monitor for anomalies.

## Testing

- List and review third-party apps and scopes; verify least privilege and approval process.
