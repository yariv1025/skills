## Summary

Unclear privacy documentation. Users cannot make informed decisions if policies are vague, long, or hidden.

## Prevention

Provide clear, accessible privacy notice and terms; explain what is collected, why, and how long. Update and communicate changes; avoid legalese where possible.

## Testing

Review clarity and accessibility of policies; verify alignment with actual practice; user testing.
