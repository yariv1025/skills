## Summary

Collecting data beyond what is necessary for the consented or stated purpose. Violates data minimization and purpose limitation.

## Prevention

Collect only what is necessary for the stated purpose; avoid optional fields that are not needed; review and prune collection; document purpose per data category.

## Testing

Audit data collection against stated purposes; verify minimization and consent alignment; review forms and tracking.
