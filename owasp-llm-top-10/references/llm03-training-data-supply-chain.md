## Summary

Risks from training data quality and supply chain (data sources, model provenance, dependencies). Malicious or biased data affects model behavior.

- Curate and verify training data sources; maintain provenance; scan for poisoning and bias. Secure model pipeline and dependencies; verify integrity of models and datasets.
